about

i'm a bengali-american artist making music out of chinatown, nyc. i've been making music since i was a kid, starting with tabla and piano and eventually finding my way to production, jazz, and the weirder corners of hip hop and electronic music. i've scored runway shows at new york fashion week, produced for other artists, and dj'd a few shows -- but the thing i'm most focused on right now is releasing original music and building this project from the ground up.
